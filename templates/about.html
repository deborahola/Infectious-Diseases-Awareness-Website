ABOUT

We hope you enjoyed our website!

We created this website to raise awareness about preventable infectious diseases in our society. We believe that everyone should be learning more about these diseases and are dedicated to educating the public about the causes, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of them. Overall, we hope to promote healthier lifestyles and provide a variety of resources that may be helpful to those in need.


This project was created by Deborah Oladokun and Yuxuan Lin during the FinTech Focus Summer 2022 Program.
